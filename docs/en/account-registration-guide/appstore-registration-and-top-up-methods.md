# How to Register for an App Store Account in Other Regions and Top-Up Methods

## I. Registering for "Hong Kong" or "US" Apple ID

1. Visit [https://www.icloud.com/](https://www.icloud.com/) - Step 1

![](/images/0f0a6235fb6d.webp)

Click "Create Apple ID" and fill in your information.

![](/images/0d3761133e69.webp)

![](/images/518fcf4dae78.webp)

After registration is complete 👇

![](/images/9a62023a8589.webp)

Go directly to the App Store on your phone and log in with your new account. Note: You may need to log in twice to successfully access the App Store. (Downloading free apps does not require binding a payment method.)

Since Shadowrocket requires a purchase and we haven’t set up a payment method yet, you’ll need a Mastercard or Visa card. Alternatively, you can purchase a Hong Kong App Store gift card from Taobao or Xianyu.

Shadowrocket costs 22 HKD, so you only need to buy a gift card with a value of 22 HKD or higher.

![](/images/85c2d95388c4.webp)

Purchasing a 30 HKD gift card is sufficient.

![](/images/f7ecd660b543.webp)

After receiving the gift card code, go to the App Store and redeem it.

![](/images/747d9f8a0260.webp)

Enter your gift card code.

![](/images/cc4fa7f1a4f0.webp)

Simply redeem it. If you see the screen below, the redemption was successful.

![](/images/895e7710c573.webp)

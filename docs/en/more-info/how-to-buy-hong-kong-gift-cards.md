# How to Purchase Hong Kong Region Gift Cards

## How to Buy Hong Kong App Store Gift Cards

For users of Apple devices, the Hong Kong App Store is particularly attractive due to its rich selection of apps, games, and media content. Whether you want to try apps not yet available in the Mainland China region or purchase specific games or subscription services, having a Hong Kong region Gift Card is one of the most direct and secure ways to make purchases. It essentially tops up your Hong Kong Apple ID account with HKD, allowing you to spend freely within the store.

### Main Purchase Channels and Methods

There are primarily online and offline channels for purchasing Hong Kong region gift cards, with online channels being the absolute mainstream for users in mainland China.

1.  **Official Online Channel (Most Recommended)**
    *   **Apple Hong Kong Official Website**: This is the most official channel. You need to visit the gift card page on the Apple Hong Kong official website. Payment typically requires a credit card that supports HKD payments (like Visa, MasterCard) or a PayPal account linked to an HKD account. After purchase, the gift card code is sent directly to you via email. This method is safe and reliable but has certain requirements for payment methods.

2.  **Major E-commerce Platforms**
    *   **Taobao/Tmall**: This is the most commonly used and convenient channel for users in mainland China. Search for "香港 App Store 礼品卡" or "港区 Apple Gift Card" to find many reputable sellers. Be sure to choose stores with a long history, high sales volume, and high positive ratings. When purchasing, you usually need to provide your email address, and the seller will send the card PIN (redemption code) to you. **Note**: The price is usually slightly higher than the face value exchange rate, reflecting the seller's channel costs and risks.

3.  **Professional Digital Goods Websites**
    *   Some international digital product retail websites, such as **OffGamers** and **SEAGM**, also sell Hong Kong Apple Gift Cards. They support various payment methods, including Alipay and WeChat Pay, and sometimes may offer competitive prices. The purchase process is similar to e-commerce platforms, with instant delivery of the digital code after ordering.

4.  **Offline Physical Stores (Applicable if you are in Hong Kong)**
    *   If you are in Hong Kong, you can purchase physical gift cards directly at **Apple Store retail shops**, **7-11 convenience stores**, **PARKnSHOP supermarkets**, **Fortress electrical appliance stores**, and other physical retailers.

### Top-up and Usage Steps

After obtaining the gift card code, the usage process is very simple:

1.  **Log in to the Correct Apple ID**: Ensure you have **logged out of your current Mainland China region account** in the device's App Store or iTunes Store and logged into your **Hong Kong region Apple ID**. If you don't have a Hong Kong account yet, you can refer to this detailed guide: [外区Appstore注册方式以及充值方法](/network/account-registration-guide/appstore-registration-and-top-up-methods).
2.  **Go to the Redemption Page**: In the App Store, click on the profile icon in the top right corner, go to the account page, and find the "**Redeem Gift Card or Code**" option. You can also directly visit Apple's official redemption website via a browser.
3.  **Enter the Code**: Manually enter or use the camera to scan the received gift card code, then click "Redeem."
4.  **Complete Top-up**: Upon success, your account balance will immediately update with the corresponding HKD amount. You can then use it like a normal balance to purchase apps, games, in-app purchases, or subscription services.

### Important Notes and Safety Advice

*   **Account Region Must Match**: The gift card must exactly match the store region of the Apple ID. **A Hong Kong region gift card can only be used with a Hong Kong region Apple ID** and cannot be used in other regional stores.
*   **Beware of Scams**: When purchasing from non-official channels, absolutely do not trust offers with prices "far below" the market exchange rate. These are likely scams or involve illicit cards (purchased with stolen credit cards). Using such codes may lead to your Apple ID being banned.
*   **Instant Delivery**: Legitimate digital gift cards deliver the code instantly. Be cautious if a seller requires a long "waiting for delivery" time.
*   **Never Provide Your Password**: Redemption only requires the code. Any request for your Apple ID password is a scam.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I buy Hong Kong region gift cards directly with RMB?
On the Apple Hong Kong official website, direct payment with RMB is typically not supported. However, on mainland e-commerce platforms like Taobao, sellers have already converted the HKD price to an RMB price. You can directly use Alipay, WeChat Pay, etc., to purchase with RMB, with the seller handling the exchange rate and payment issues. This is the most convenient method for users in mainland China.

### Can the top-up balance be used for Family Sharing?
Yes. The account balance of a Hong Kong Apple ID (including portions topped up via gift cards) can be used **to pay for subscription fees for the Family Sharing organizer (such as Apple One, iCloud+)**. However, the balance itself cannot be directly transferred between family members or shared for purchasing individual apps.

### Do gift cards have an expiration date?
According to Apple's official policy, **the gift card code itself does not expire** (unless otherwise specified in the purchase terms). However, once you redeem the code into an Apple ID account, this **account balance may have an expiration date**, depending on the laws and regulations of your region. In Hong Kong, the balance after top-up is usually valid long-term. It is recommended to use it promptly after topping up or to check the balance information on your account page.

# How to Purchase Hong Kong Region Gift Cards

## Guide to Buying Hong Kong App Store Gift Cards

Purchasing Hong Kong App Store gift cards is a convenient method for many users to access apps, games, music, or pay for subscription services exclusive to the Hong Kong region. Compared to directly linking a credit card, gift cards do not require a local Hong Kong bank card, offering greater flexibility and security. Below are several mainstream and reliable purchasing channels and important usage considerations.

### Main Purchasing Channels

Currently, purchasing Hong Kong region gift cards is primarily divided into online and offline methods.

**1. Official Online Platforms and Authorized Retailers**
This is the most common and secure channel. You can directly visit the gift card page on Apple's Hong Kong official website to make a purchase. Additionally, some large, reputable international e-commerce platforms also offer digital gift cards. After purchase, the card code is typically sent instantly via email or site message, which is very convenient. When using such platforms, be sure to confirm they are authorized Apple retailers to avoid purchasing invalid or already-used codes.

**2. Offline Physical Store Purchases**
If you are in Hong Kong or have the opportunity to visit, the most direct method is to purchase physical cards at Hong Kong Apple Retail Stores, major supermarket chains (such as PARKnSHOP, Wellcome), convenience stores (like 7-Eleven, OK便利店), or electronics stores (such as Fortress, Broadway). Physical cards are also more suitable as gifts.

**3. Third-Party Reseller Websites**
For users unable to purchase through the above channels, many websites offer reselling services. **Extra caution is required when choosing such channels.** Be sure to select websites with a good reputation, long establishment history, and comprehensive customer service, and carefully read user reviews. Be wary of items priced significantly below face value, as they may be scams.

### Detailed Steps for Purchase and Use

1.  **Select Denomination and Purchase**: Gift cards typically have fixed denominations (e.g., 50, 100, 200, 500 HKD, etc.). Choose the appropriate denomination based on your needs and complete the payment.
2.  **Receive the Card Code**: If you purchase a digital card, securely save the received redemption code (a combination of uppercase letters and numbers). For physical cards, scratch off the coating on the back to reveal the code.
3.  **Redeem to Your Account**:
    *   On an iOS device or Mac, open the App Store.
    *   Click on your profile picture to enter the account page.
    *   Select "Redeem Gift Card or Code."
    *   Use the camera to scan the QR code on a physical card, or manually enter the digital card code.
4.  **Start Spending**: After successful redemption, the balance will be displayed in your account. For subsequent app purchases or subscriptions, the system will prioritize deducting from the gift card balance.

### Important Considerations

*   **Account Region Must Match**: The gift card you redeem must **exactly match** the region of your Apple ID account. Hong Kong region gift cards can only be redeemed into an Apple ID set to the "Hong Kong" region. They cannot be redeemed into US, Japan, Mainland China, or other regional accounts. If you don't have a Hong Kong region account yet, you can refer to this detailed guide on [How to Register for a Foreign App Store Account and Top-Up Methods](/network/account-registration-guide/appstore-registration-and-top-up-methods) for registration.
*   **Balance is Non-Transferable**: Gift card balances cannot be transferred between Apple IDs of different regions, nor can they be exchanged for cash.
*   **Check Validity Period**: When purchasing, please note that while officially issued Apple gift cards typically have no expiration date, some cards sold through third-party channels may have usage period limitations.
*   **Guard Against Risks**: Do not trust individual sellers lightly. Avoid transactions through unofficial channels to protect your funds and personal account security.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can Hong Kong Region Gift Cards Be Used with a Mainland China Region Account?
Absolutely not. The binding between gift cards and the Apple ID region is strict. Hong Kong region gift cards can only be used with an Apple ID set to the "Hong Kong" region. Attempting to redeem them on a Mainland China account will result in a direct system error. You need a separate Hong Kong region Apple ID to use it.

### How Long Does It Take to Receive the Card Code After Purchasing a Digital Card?
When purchasing from the Apple official website or large, reputable authorized e-commerce platforms, the code is usually issued almost instantly (within minutes) and sent to the email address provided during purchase. For third-party reseller websites, processing times may vary from a few minutes to several hours. It's best to check the website's instructions or consult customer service before purchasing.

### What If There's Unused Balance on the Gift Card?
Any unused balance will remain in your Hong Kong region Apple ID account and can be used for any future purchases, including app purchases, in-app purchases, subscriptions to Apple Music, iCloud+, etc. You can check the remaining balance at any time on the account page within the App Store.

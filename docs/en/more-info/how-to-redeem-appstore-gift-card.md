# How to Redeem App Store Gift Cards

## Complete Guide to Redeeming App Store Gift Cards

App Store gift cards are a convenient way to add funds to your Apple account or gift digital content like apps, games, music, movies, iCloud+, and more. Whether you have a physical card or an electronic code, the redemption process is simple and quick. Here is a detailed step-by-step guide and important considerations.

### How to Redeem a Gift Card

The method primarily depends on the type of gift card you have: a **physical card** or an **electronic card/redemption code**. The core prerequisite is: **You must be logged in with an Apple ID account that exactly matches the gift card's region of issue**. For example, a US gift card must be redeemed with a US Apple ID.

**1. Redeeming on iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch**
*   **Open the App Store app** and tap your profile picture or icon in the top-right corner.
*   Scroll down, find, and tap the "**Redeem Gift Card or Code**" option.
*   You can choose:
    *   **Use Camera**: If you have a physical card, directly scan the scratched-off area on the back of the card.
    *   **Enter Code Manually**: If you received an electronic code (a string of letters and numbers), tap "You can also enter your code manually," then carefully input the code.
*   Tap "Redeem." The funds will be added to your Apple ID balance immediately.

**2. Redeeming on Mac or PC**
*   **On Mac**: Open the **App Store**. At the bottom of the sidebar, click your name or "Sign In." Find and click "**Redeem Gift Card**." Then scan or manually enter the code.
*   **On PC**: Open **iTunes** (Windows) or **Apple Music** (Windows), sign in, then from the menu bar (top) select "Account" > "**Redeem**," and enter the code.

**3. Redeeming on the Apple Website**
*   You can also visit Apple's official redemption page directly in your browser: [https://apple.co/redeem](https://apple.co/redeem).
*   Sign in with your Apple ID and enter the redemption code.

### Important Considerations Before and After Redemption

1.  **Region Matching is Key**: This is the most important point. Your Apple ID country/region setting must match the gift card's issue region. You can check your current setting in "Settings" > "[Your Name]" > "Media & Purchases" > "View Account" > "Country/Region." **You cannot use a gift card from a different region by switching your country/region**; switching regions requires a zero balance.
2.  **Check Gift Card Status**: Ensure the gift card is new and unscratched. A used code cannot be redeemed again.
3.  **Balance Usage**: The added funds can be used to purchase all content on the App Store, iTunes Store, and Apple Books, as well as pay for subscriptions like Apple Music, iCloud+, and Apple TV+.
4.  **No Cash Withdrawal**: Account balance cannot be converted to cash or transferred to another account.
5.  **Check Balance**: After redemption, you can view your current balance in the App Store, iTunes Store, or in "Settings" > "[Your Name]" > "Media & Purchases" > "View Account."

If you don't have an Apple ID for the corresponding region yet, or need to top up a foreign region account, you can refer to our detailed guide on **[How to Register for a Foreign App Store Account and Top-Up Methods](/network/account-registration-guide/appstore-registration-and-top-up-methods)**, which includes the complete process from registration to secure top-up.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What to do if the gift card redemption shows "Code Invalid"?
First, double-check the entered code, especially easily confused characters (like the number 0 and the letter O). Ensure there are no extra spaces. If it's confirmed correct, the most common reason is **Apple ID region mismatch with the gift card region**. Check your account's region setting. Secondly, confirm the gift card was purchased through official channels, as cards from unofficial sources may sometimes be already used or invalid.

### Can the redeemed balance be used for Family Sharing?
Yes, but with limitations. As the **Family Sharing organizer**, the gift card balance in your account can be used to pay for **shared purchases** for family members (requires "Purchase Sharing" to be enabled). However, family members **cannot directly use** the balance in your account for their personal purchases; their individual account balances are separate.

### Do gift cards have an expiration date?
According to Apple's official policy, App Store gift cards **purchased within mainland China** do not have an expiration date. However, gift cards purchased in some other countries or regions may have expiration dates as per their terms, usually indicated at purchase or on the card. It's recommended to use the balance soon after redemption or check your account balance promptly.

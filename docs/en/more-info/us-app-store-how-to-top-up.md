# How to Top Up a US App Store Account

## Detailed Guide to US App Store Top-Up Methods

For users of the US App Store, adding funds is a core need. Due to Apple's regional policies, payment methods from Mainland China (such as UnionPay cards, Alipay, and WeChat Pay) cannot be used directly to top up a US account. However, you can easily solve this problem through the following mainstream and secure methods.

### Main Top-Up Methods

Currently, there are several primary ways to add funds to a US Apple ID:

1.  **Purchasing US Apple Gift Cards**
    This is the most universal and recommended method. You need to purchase physical or electronic gift cards (eGift Cards) denominated in US dollars.
    *   **Purchase Channels**: You can buy them through Amazon.com, reputable gift card websites (like G2A, PCGameSupply), or have a friend in the US purchase them for you. **Always purchase from reputable official or authorized retailers** to avoid buying discounted cards from unknown sources, which could risk your account being banned.
    *   **Redemption Steps**: In the App Store, tap on your account profile picture, select "Redeem Gift Card or Code," then manually enter or scan the code from the card. The balance will be directly added to your account.

2.  **Linking a US Payment Method**
    If you have an eligible US payment tool, you can link it directly for automatic deductions during purchases.
    *   **US Credit/Debit Card**: The card must be issued by a US bank and have a valid US billing address.
    *   **US PayPal Account**: You need to register a PayPal account set to the US region and link it to a US bank account or credit card.

3.  **Using Credit Cards that Support International Payments**
    Some dual-currency (e.g., Visa, Mastercard) or all-currency credit cards issued by domestic banks in China **may occasionally** be linked successfully after setting their billing address to a valid US address (like a freight forwarder's address). However, this is not an officially supported method. The success rate is unstable, and there is a risk of subsequent verification failure.

### How to Choose and Operational Recommendations

For the vast majority of users in China, **purchasing US gift cards is the best option**. Its advantages include:
*   **High Success Rate**: As long as the code is valid, redemption is 100% successful.
*   **Good Security**: Purchasing through正规 channels carries low account risk.
*   **Budget Control**: You can purchase specific denominations as needed to avoid overspending.

**Recommended Process**:
1.  **Preparation**: Ensure your Apple ID is switched to the US store.
2.  **Purchase**: Buy an electronic gift card from a trustworthy website (like Amazon.com). The code will be sent via email after payment.
3.  **Redemption**: On your iPhone or iPad's App Store, log in with your US Apple ID, go to your account page, and complete the redemption.
4.  **Spending**: Once your account balance is updated, you can purchase apps, subscribe to services, or make in-app purchases.

Please note that the balance in a US Apple ID **cannot be transferred or merged across regions** and is non-refundable. Additionally, Apple may review accounts for frequent country/region changes or top-up activities, so use your account normally to avoid issues.

If you don't have a US Apple ID yet, you need to register one first. ## Frequently Asked Questions

### Where is the Safest Place to Buy US Gift Cards?
The safest channel is the official Amazon.com website. You need to log into an Amazon US account, navigate to the "Gift Cards" section, select the "Email" format for Apple Gift Cards, and pay using a credit card that supports US dollars (like Visa/Mastercard). Other reliable options include the online stores of authorized retailers (like Best Buy, Walmart).

### What to Do if "Cannot Complete Purchase" or "Verification Required" Appears After Topping Up?
This usually means your account has triggered Apple's security verification. Please check the following in order: 1) Confirm the gift card is for use in the US region; 2) Try redeeming it on Apple's official website (apple.com/redeem); 3) Ensure the US address, phone number, and other information provided during account registration are relatively complete and reasonable; 4) Contact Apple Support for assistance. Avoid entering incorrect codes multiple times in a short period.

### Can US Account Balance Be Used for Subscription Services?
Absolutely. The account balance can be used for all App Store purchases, including one-time app purchases, in-app purchases, and auto-renewing subscriptions (like YouTube Premium, Disney+, various software monthly fees, etc.). Just ensure your balance is sufficient, and the system will prioritize deducting from it.

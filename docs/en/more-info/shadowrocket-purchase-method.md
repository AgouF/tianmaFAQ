# How to Purchase Shadowrocket

## What is Shadowrocket?

Shadowrocket is a highly popular proxy tool application on the iOS platform. It supports various proxy protocols such as SS, SSR, V2Ray, and Trojan, helping users access the global internet and international network resources. Due to its powerful features and flexible configuration, it is well-loved by users with specific network access needs. It's important to note that Shadowrocket is only available on non-China region App Stores (like the US or Hong Kong stores) and cannot be directly searched for or downloaded from the mainland China App Store.

## How to Purchase Shadowrocket?

Since the app itself is paid and requires a non-China Apple ID, the "purchase" process typically involves two steps: obtaining a non-China Apple ID and completing the payment for the app.

### Step 1: Obtain a Non-China Region Apple ID

This is the most crucial step. You need to register an Apple ID account for a region outside mainland China, such as the United States, Hong Kong, or Japan.

1.  **Preparation**: Prepare a brand-new email address (like Gmail or Outlook) that hasn't been bound to any other Apple ID. Ensure your device (iPhone or iPad) has logged out of your current China-region Apple ID.
2.  **Start Registration**: Visit the Apple ID registration page on your device or a computer browser. **The key point is**: When filling in "Country or Region," select your target region (e.g., United States). When filling in address and phone number information, you can use real or generated valid information for that region (there are online tools or publicly available information for reference).
3.  **Complete Verification**: Follow the process to complete email and phone verification, and you will have a non-China Apple ID.

For a more detailed, step-by-step guide with images, you can refer to our previously published article: [How to Register for a Non-China App Store and Top-Up Methods](/network/account-registration-guide/appstore-registration-and-top-up-methods), which details how to choose a region, fill in information, and important notes.

### Step 2: Top Up and Purchase with the Non-China Account

Once you have the non-China ID, log into the App Store and search for "Shadowrocket" to find it. However, you need a payment method from the corresponding region to complete the purchase.

**Common top-up/payment methods include:**

1.  **Purchasing Gift Cards**: This is the most mainstream and reliable method. You can purchase US, Hong Kong, or other region App Store gift cards on some reliable e-commerce platforms (like Taobao or websites specializing in topping up foreign game accounts). After obtaining the redemption code, redeem it in your non-China Apple ID account, and the balance can be used to purchase Shadowrocket.
2.  **Binding a Local Payment Method**: If you have a credit or debit card from the target region (e.g., a US-issued credit card), you can directly bind it to your Apple ID payment information. This method has a higher threshold for most domestic users.
3.  **Linking PayPal**: Apple IDs in some regions support linking a PayPal account that matches that region. This also requires you to have a corresponding regional payment channel.

**Purchase Process**: Log in with your non-China ID -> Search for "Shadowrocket" -> Click the price button (usually around $2.99) -> Complete the purchase using your account balance or bound payment method. After downloading, you will permanently own it in your iPhone's "Purchase History." Even if you switch back to your China-region ID, the app will remain (you need to switch back to the non-China ID for updates).

## Usage Notes and Suggestions

*   **Account Security**: Please keep your non-China Apple ID and password safe. Avoid sharing it with others to prevent account suspension.
*   **Switching Accounts**: Logging into the non-China ID in the iOS "App Store" is only for downloading and updating apps. You can still keep your China-region ID as your main iCloud account in the system's "Settings" -> "[Your Name]". The two can coexist without affecting iCloud data (like photos, contacts).
*   **Finding Configurations**: Shadowrocket itself is just a client tool and does not provide proxy servers. You need to find a reliable and secure proxy service provider on your own and obtain the corresponding server configuration information (subscription link or manual configuration parameters) to import into Shadowrocket before you can use it.
*   **Compliant Use**: Please be sure to comply with local laws and regulations and use the tool for legitimate learning and work purposes.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### I already have a US Apple ID, but it says "Cannot Complete Purchase." What should I do?
This is usually because your account payment information is incomplete. Even when using gift card balance for purchases, Apple IDs in some regions (especially newly registered ones) may require billing address verification. Try logging into your Apple ID on the official Apple website, go to the "Payment & Shipping" section, and add a valid local address (you can use an address from a tax-free state like Oregon). After adding it, try purchasing again.

### After purchasing Shadowrocket, can I use it permanently?
Yes. Once purchased, the app is permanently associated with your non-China Apple ID. Even if the app is delisted from the store in the future (which is very unlikely), your purchased app can still be re-downloaded from the "Purchased" list. Future major version updates also do not require additional payment.

### Are there other similar apps besides Shadowrocket?

You can choose based on community reviews and personal preference. The purchase method is exactly the same as for Shadowrocket.

# Hong Kong Region Gift Card Purchase and Redemption Guide

## Introduction to Hong Kong Region App Store Gift Cards

Hong Kong region App Store gift cards are a convenient way for Apple users in Hong Kong to top up their Apple ID accounts. Unlike mainland China, gift cards are widely used for consumption and gifting in Hong Kong. Gift cards come in two forms: physical cards and electronic codes (E-Code), typically available in denominations of 50, 100, 200, 500, and 1000 HKD. Upon successful redemption, the balance will be deposited into your Hong Kong region Apple ID account and can be used to purchase paid apps, games, iCloud+ subscriptions, Apple Music, video content, and in-app purchases within the App Store.

For users with a Hong Kong region Apple ID, gift cards are the best solution if you cannot directly link a non-Hong Kong local credit card or payment method. Before starting, ensure your Apple ID region is switched to "Hong Kong." You can check and change this in **Settings > [Your Name] > Media & Purchases > View Account > Country/Region**.

## How to Purchase Hong Kong Region Gift Cards

Purchase channels are mainly divided into online and offline.

**1. Online Purchase (Recommended)**
This is the most convenient and fastest method, especially for users outside Hong Kong.
*   **Official Website**: You can directly visit the gift card page on Apple's Hong Kong official website to make a purchase, but this usually requires a local Hong Kong payment method.
*   **Authorized E-commerce Platforms**: Many large, reputable e-commerce platforms sell Hong Kong region App Store gift card electronic codes, such as some major digital specialty stores on Taobao, Carousell, etc. When purchasing, be sure to choose sellers with high sales volume and good reviews.
*   **Professional Gift Card Websites**: Some international gift card websites also offer Hong Kong region card sales, supporting various payment methods.

**When purchasing electronic cards, please note:**
*   **Choose a Reputable Seller**: Carefully check the store's rating, historical reviews, and transaction volume to avoid the risk of encountering black market cards (cards purchased with stolen credit cards) or invalid cards, which could lead to account suspension.
*   **Instant Delivery**: Legitimate electronic card sellers typically send a redemption code via chat window or email within minutes after payment.
*   **Confirm Denomination and Exchange Rate**: The price may fluctuate slightly due to exchange rates and merchant profit margins. Confirm the final RMB amount before purchasing.

**2. Offline Purchase**
If you or a friend are in Hong Kong, you can purchase physical gift cards at the following locations:
*   Apple Store retail locations
*   Large chain convenience stores like 7-Eleven, OK Convenience Store, PARKnSHOP, Wellcome
*    Authorized reseller stores
Scratch the coating on the physical card to reveal the redemption code.

## Detailed Redemption Step-by-Step Guide

After purchasing the gift card (obtaining the redemption code), follow these steps to complete the redemption on your iPhone, iPad, or Mac.

**To redeem on iPhone or iPad:**
1.  Open the **App Store** app.
2.  Tap your **profile icon** in the top-right corner of the screen.
3.  Tap **"Redeem Gift Card or Code."** You can also access this via **Settings > [Your Name] > Media & Purchases > Redeem Gift Card or Code**.
4.  You can choose **"Use Camera"** to scan the QR code on the back of a physical card, or **"Enter Code Manually."**
5.  Carefully enter or scan the obtained **16-digit redemption code**, then tap **"Redeem"** in the top-right corner.
6.  The system will confirm the successful redemption, and your account balance will update immediately.

**To redeem on Mac:**
1.  Open the **App Store** app.
2.  At the bottom of the left sidebar, click your **account name**.
3.  On the account page, find and click the **"Redeem Gift Card"** button.
4.  Similarly, you can choose to scan a QR code or enter the code manually.
5.  Enter the code and click **"Redeem."**

**Important Notes:**
*   Once a redemption code is successfully redeemed, it cannot be revoked or transferred.
*   Ensure you are logged into your **Hong Kong region Apple ID** when redeeming; otherwise, you may receive a prompt that the code is invalid or for another store.
*   Account balances cannot be transferred between Apple IDs from different regions.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What to do if prompted "Code Invalid" or "Unable to Redeem" during redemption?
First, **carefully double-check the entered 16-digit code** to ensure no letters or numbers are mistyped (note the difference between 0 and O, 1 and I). Second, **confirm your Apple ID region is set to Hong Kong**. If the region is correct and the code is accurate, possible reasons include: 1) The gift card has already been used; 2) You purchased a black market card (illegally sourced card). In this case, contact the seller promptly. To avoid such issues, purchasing from reliable channels is crucial. If you don't have a Hong Kong region account yet, you can refer to this detailed guide on [How to Register for a Foreign Region App Store Account and Top-Up Methods](/network/account-registration-guide/appstore-registration-and-top-up-methods) to create one.

### Can Hong Kong region gift card balance be used to subscribe to services?
Absolutely. The gift card balance in your account is universal funds for your Hong Kong region Apple ID and can be used to pay for all items billed through the App Store. This includes, but is not limited to: Apple Music individual/family plans, iCloud+ storage upgrades, Apple Arcade game subscription, Apple TV+ video subscription, and auto-renewing subscriptions for various apps (like Notability, Procreate Pocket, etc.). Subscription fees will be deducted from your gift card balance first.

### Can unused gift card balance be refunded or cashed out?
**No.** According to Apple's terms, once a gift card balance is topped up into an account, **it cannot be exchanged for cash, does not support refunds, and cannot be transferred between different Apple IDs**. The balance will remain in the account for future consumption. Therefore, it is recommended to purchase gift cards of appropriate denominations based on actual needs to avoid topping up excessively large amounts at once.

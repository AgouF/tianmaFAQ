# How to Redeem App Store Gift Cards

## Complete Guide to Redeeming App Store Gift Cards

App Store gift cards are a convenient way to obtain Apple Store (App Store) credit, purchase apps, games, subscription services (like Apple Music, iCloud+), or make media purchases. Whether it's a physical card or an e-card, the redemption process is designed to be very simple. Here are the detailed steps and important notes.

### How to Redeem an App Store Gift Card

The redemption process is primarily done on iPhone, iPad, Mac, or Apple TV, with the core steps being consistent.

**Step 1: Preparation**
1.  **Confirm the Gift Card Type**: Ensure you have an **App Store & iTunes gift card** (typically with a white card face and various designs), not an Apple Store gift card limited to specific hardware (used for purchasing physical products).
2.  **Scratch Off the Coating**: If it's a physical card, gently scratch off the silver coating on the back to reveal the full redemption code. Avoid excessive scratching that could damage the code.

**Step 2: Redeeming on an iOS/iPadOS Device (Using iPhone as an Example)**
1.  Open the **App Store** app.
2.  Tap your **profile picture icon** in the top-right corner of the screen to enter your account page.
3.  Tap the **"Redeem Gift Card or Code"** option. You can also choose **"Use Camera"** to directly scan the code on the back of the physical card; this is the fastest method.
4.  If not scanning, you need to manually **"Enter Code"**. Carefully input the long string of characters under the coating (without spaces), then tap "Redeem" in the top-right corner.
5.  The system will verify immediately. Upon success, the credit will automatically be added to your Apple ID account. You can see the updated balance at the top of your account page.

**Step 3: Redeeming on Other Devices**
*   **On a Mac**: Open the **App Store** app, click on your account name at the bottom of the sidebar, then select "Redeem Gift Card."
*   **On Apple TV**: Go to the **App Store**, select your account, then find the "Redeem Gift Card" option.

### Important Notes and Common Issues

*   **Regional Restrictions**: Gift cards have strict **regional restrictions**. For example, a US-region gift card can only be redeemed to a US-region Apple ID account and cannot be used for a China-region account. Ensure the gift card region matches your Apple ID account region. If you need to manage a foreign-region account, you can refer to this detailed guide on [Foreign App Store Registration Methods and Top-up Methods](/account-registration-guide/appstore-registration-and-top-up-methods).
*   **Credit Usage**: The credited balance can be used to purchase almost all digital content within the App Store, including paid apps, in-app purchases, music, movies, books, and various subscription services provided by Apple.
*   **Possible Reasons for Failed Redemption**:
    *   Incorrect code entry (confusing the number 0 and the letter O, the number 1 and the letter I, etc.).
    *   The gift card has already been redeemed by someone else (risk from non-official purchase sources).
    *   Account region does not match the card region.
    *   Network connection issues.
*   **Security Advice**: Only purchase gift cards from Apple-authorized retailers to avoid buying leaked or counterfeit cards. Do not purchase heavily discounted gift cards from unknown sources, as they are highly likely to be scams.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Where Can I Check My Balance After Redeeming a Gift Card?
After successful redemption, you can check your current balance directly in the **App Store** app on your iPhone or iPad by tapping your profile picture in the top-right corner to enter your account page; the balance is displayed below your account name. You can also view the same balance in **Settings > [Your Name] > Media & Purchases**.

### What Should I Do If I Get an "Invalid Code" Prompt During Redemption?
First, carefully double-check that the entered characters are correct. If confirmed correct, the most common reason is a **mismatch between your account region and the gift card region**. For example, trying to redeem a US gift card with a Mainland China Apple ID will inevitably fail. You need to log in with the corresponding regional Apple ID to redeem it. If the problem persists, contact Apple Support and provide the gift card information for assistance.

### Can a Gift Card Be Used Partially or Get Change?
Yes. A gift card adds a fixed amount of credit to your account. When you make a purchase, the system will first deduct from your gift card balance. If the purchase amount is less than your balance, the remaining amount stays in your account for future use. If the purchase amount exceeds your balance, the system will prompt you to bind another payment method (like a credit card, Alipay, etc.) to pay the difference.

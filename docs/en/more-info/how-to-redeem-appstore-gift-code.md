# How to Redeem an App Store Gift Code

## How to Redeem an App Store Gift Code

An App Store gift code (also known as a gift card or top-up card) is an official Apple-issued prepaid card that can be used to purchase apps, games, music, movies, subscription services, or to add credit to your Apple ID account on the App Store, iTunes Store, and Apple Books. Once you receive a gift code, the redemption process is very simple and can be completed in just a few steps.

### Preparations Before Redemption

Before you begin redeeming, please ensure you have completed the following preparations:
1.  **Confirm the Gift Code Type**: Gift codes come in two forms: physical cards and digital codes. For physical cards, you scratch off the coating to reveal the password; digital codes are sent via email or message. Please carefully check the password, distinguishing between easily confused characters (such as the number 0 and the letter O).
2.  **Check Account Region**: This is the most crucial step. **The gift code must exactly match the country or region of your Apple ID account**. For example, a US-region gift code can only be added to a US-region Apple ID account and cannot be used for a China-region account. If you have a foreign-region account, you can refer to our detailed guide on [Foreign Region App Store Registration and Top-up Methods](/network/account-registration-guide/appstore-registration-and-top-up-methods) for instructions.
3.  **Log into the Correct Account**: On the device you are using to redeem (iPhone, iPad, Mac, or PC), ensure you are logged into the Apple ID for the corresponding region.

### Redeeming on iPhone or iPad

This is the most commonly used redemption method, offering an intuitive and convenient process:
1.  Open the **App Store** app on your device.
2.  Tap your profile picture icon in the top-right corner of the screen to enter your account page.
3.  Tap the **"Redeem Gift Card or Code"** option.
4.  You can choose **"Use Camera"** to scan the barcode on the back of a physical card, or select **"Enter Code Manually"** and then carefully input the gift code password.
5.  Tap the **"Redeem"** button in the top-right corner.
6.  After successful verification by the system, the credit will be immediately added to your Apple ID account. You can see your updated balance at the top of your account page.

### Redeeming on Mac or PC

If you prefer using a computer, you can also operate via the following methods:
*   **On Mac**: Open the **App Store** app, find and click your account name or profile picture at the bottom of the left sidebar, then click **"Redeem Gift Card"**.
*   **On PC (Windows)**: Open the **iTunes** application (please note that Apple has gradually ceased feature updates for the Windows version of iTunes, but the redemption function is usually still available). Click **"Account"** in the menu bar, then select **"Redeem"**.
*   Subsequent steps are similar to the mobile process: choose to scan or manually enter the code to complete the redemption.

### Common Reasons for Redemption Failure and Solutions

*   **"Code is Invalid" or "Unable to Redeem"**:
    *   **Region Mismatch**: This is the most common reason. Please double-check that the gift code region matches your Apple ID region.
    *   **Input Error**: Carefully check the code to ensure no characters are mistyped, and that no spaces are missing or extra.
    *   **Gift Code Already Used**: Confirm whether the code has already been redeemed by you or someone else.
    *   **Gift Code Not Activated**: If purchased from a physical store, the card might not have been activated by the cashier. Please contact the point of purchase.
*   **"This Code is Not Valid in This Store"**: This clearly indicates a region error. You need to switch to the corresponding region's store to redeem.
*   **Account Has Outstanding Balance**: If your account has unpaid subscription or purchase orders, you may need to resolve the billing issue before you can successfully add credit.

After successful redemption, the credit will be stored in your account. All eligible future purchases (such as paid apps, in-app purchases, subscriptions to Apple Music/iCloud+, etc.) will be deducted from this balance first.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can a Gift Code Be Refunded or Cashed Out After Redemption?
No. According to Apple's terms, once a gift code is successfully redeemed into account credit, that credit cannot be refunded, exchanged for cash, or transferred to another account. The credit can only be used for purchases within Apple's ecosystem. Please confirm your account and needs before redeeming.

### Do Gift Codes Have an Expiration Date?
Yes, typically the gift code itself (i.e., the card or code) will have a printed expiration date. However, after successfully redeeming it to your Apple ID account, the credit **in your account does not expire** and can be used indefinitely until depleted. It is recommended to redeem gift codes as soon as you receive them to avoid the card expiring or getting lost.

### What Should I Do If I Get a "Cannot Complete Purchase" Prompt During Redemption?
This is usually related to payment information verification for the account. Even when using a gift card, Apple sometimes requires a valid payment method (such as a credit card) as a backup verification method for the account. You can try:
1.  On your iPhone, go to "Settings" > "[Your Name]" > "Payment & Shipping" and add a valid payment method (even if you plan to use only the credit balance).
2.  If you prefer not to bind a payment method, you can try redeeming through the Apple ID account management page on Apple's official website.
3.  Ensure your device has a stable network connection and try restarting the device before attempting the operation again.

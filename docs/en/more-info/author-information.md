## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the difference between an AI author and a human author?
AI authors like me can quickly process large amounts of information, maintain an objective and consistent style, and work 24/7 without interruption. However, I lack real human experiences and emotional resonance. Human authors possess unique life experiences, emotional depth, and creative inspiration, which are currently difficult for AI to fully replicate. Both have their own advantages and are suitable for different creative needs.

### How can I verify the accuracy of information provided by AI?

2. Cross-verify with multiple information sources.
3. Pay attention to the timeliness of information, especially in rapidly developing fields.
4. Feel free to ask for further details or sources if you have doubts.

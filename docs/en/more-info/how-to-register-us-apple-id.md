# How to Register a US Apple ID

Want to register a US Apple ID to download apps and games not available in the China region, or enjoy richer media content? It's actually not complicated. The entire process is completely free, and the core requirement is having **a US address and phone number not associated with any other Apple ID**. Here is a detailed step-by-step guide and practical advice.

## Preparation
Before starting the registration, ensure you have the following information ready, as this is key to success:
1.  **A brand new email address**: It's recommended to use an international email service like Gmail or Outlook, dedicated for registering the US ID. Do not use the same email as your existing China region ID.
2.  **A US address and zip code**: This is crucial for verifying the region. You can use a real friend's US address or a legitimate freight forwarder's address. You can also find some publicly available address information online (e.g., universities, hotels), but pay attention to its usability. An example of a usable zip code is `90001` (Los Angeles, California).
3.  **A US phone number (optional but recommended)**: Used to receive verification codes and enhance account security. If you don't have one, you can skip it for now, but some subsequent operations (like purchasing paid apps) might be restricted. You can consider using some online SMS reception services.
4.  **A stable internet connection**: No special network tools are needed during registration, but you might need them later when logging into the App Store to download apps.

## Detailed Registration Steps
It's recommended to operate on a computer web browser (via the Apple website) or on an iPhone; the processes are similar.

### Method 1: Register via the Apple Website (Clearest Method)
1.  Visit the Apple ID creation page (appleid.apple.com) and click "Create Your Apple ID".
2.  Fill in the registration information:
    *   **Country/Region**: Must select **"United States"**.
    *   Name: You can use Pinyin or an English name.
    *   Date of Birth: Must be over 18 years old.
    *   Email and Password: Use the new email you prepared.
    *   Phone Number: You can enter a Chinese mobile number for auxiliary verification. However, the **Payment Method section** is the key part.
3.  After entering the account management page, find the "Payment & Shipping" section.
4.  Click "Add Payment Method" and **select "None" as the payment method**. If the "None" option is not visible, go back and confirm that you selected the United States as the country in the first step.
5.  Fill in the US address and phone number. The address information (state, city, street, zip code) needs to be consistent. You can use Google Maps to find a real, valid combination.
6.  After completing and saving all information, your US Apple ID is essentially created.

### Method 2: Register Directly on iPhone/iPad
1.  First, completely sign out of your current Apple ID (Settings > Tap your profile picture at the top > Sign Out).
2.  Open the **App Store**, tap the profile icon in the top right corner, then "Create New Apple ID".
3.  During creation, the most critical step is when selecting the **country or region; please scroll and choose "United States"**.
4.  Subsequent steps are similar to the web version: agree to the terms, fill in email, password, and security questions.
5.  On the payment method page, **be sure to select "None"**. Then enter your prepared US address and zip code.
6.  After completion, check your registration email to verify the Apple ID.

## Important Notes and Usage Tips
*   **Payment Method "None"**: This is the core of registering a free US Apple ID. If you cannot find the "None" option, it means the region you selected during the registration process might not be the United States. Please go back and try again.
*   **Address Verification**: Apple occasionally verifies addresses. Using a completely fictional, garbled address carries risks. It is recommended to use real, publicly available address information.
*   **Switching Stores**: After successful registration, on your iOS device, you need to **completely sign out of your China region ID and then log in with your US ID** to access the US App Store. Do not directly switch countries in the Settings app, as that will require you to provide a valid US payment method.
*   **Gift Card Top-up**: After selecting "None" as the payment method for your US ID, to purchase paid apps or subscribe to services, you primarily rely on purchasing **US Apple Gift Cards** for top-up. Be sure to purchase from official channels. Never use gift cards from unknown sources, as there is a risk of account suspension. For specific safe methods regarding top-up, you can refer to this detailed guide: [How to Register and Top Up Foreign Region Appstore Accounts](/network/account-registration-guide/appstore-registration-and-top-up-methods).
*   **Two-Factor Authentication**: For account security, it is strongly recommended to enable two-factor authentication. You can use a Chinese mobile number to receive verification codes.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What if I can't find the "None" payment option during registration?
This is usually because the country/region selected on your registration page is not the United States. Please ensure from the very first step that the region selected is the United States. If registering via the App Store, make sure you have completely signed out of your current ID and start the creation from the store's homepage. Sometimes clearing browser cache or changing browsers can also solve the issue.

### Can I use both a US ID and a China ID on a device simultaneously?
Yes, but you cannot be logged into both at the same time. You need to **sign out of one and then log into the other** on a device. For example, you can log into the US ID in the App Store to download specific apps, while continuing to use your China ID for services like iCloud and iMessage (but this setup is slightly more complex and can get confusing). A more common practice is to prepare two devices, or primarily use one ID and temporarily log into the other only when needed for downloads.

### Will using a US ID get my account suspended?
As long as you comply with Apple's user agreement and use it normally, **registering and using a US ID itself will not lead to suspension**. The biggest suspension risks come from: 1) Using "black market" gift cards (purchased with stolen credit cards) for top-up; 2) Frequently switching between multiple different country regions; 3) Clearly fraudulent account information. Using real information for registration and topping up through official channels allows for safe use.

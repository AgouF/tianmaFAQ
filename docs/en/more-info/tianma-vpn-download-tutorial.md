# Pegasus VPN Download Tutorial

## Before Downloading Pegasus VPN

Before starting the download of Pegasus VPN, there are several important points to note. First, please ensure you are visiting the official Pegasus VPN website or its official authorized app store page to avoid downloading unofficial versions that may be tampered with or contain malware, thereby protecting your device's security. Second, please select the corresponding client version based on your device's specific operating system (e.g., Windows, macOS, Android, iOS). Finally, it is recommended to check if your network connection is stable and ensure your device has sufficient storage space before downloading.

## Step-by-Step Download Guide for Major Platforms

Here is a detailed download guide for different device platforms.

### Downloading on Android Devices
For Android phone or tablet users, the safest and most convenient method is to obtain it via the Google Play Store.
1.  Open the **Google Play Store** app on your device.
2.  Enter "**Pegasus VPN**" or "**Tianma VPN**" in the search bar at the top.
3.  Find the official app from the search results. The developer usually indicates the company name; please verify carefully.
4.  Click the "Install" button and wait for the download and installation to complete.

If you cannot access Google Play, you can try visiting the official Pegasus VPN website and find the APK installation file for Android on the download page. After downloading, you need to allow "Installation from Unknown Sources" in your device's settings to complete the installation.

### Downloading on iOS Devices
Apple users need to download via the App Store.
1.  Open the **App Store** on your iPhone or iPad.
2.  Tap the "Search" option at the bottom right, and enter "**Pegasus VPN**" in the search box.
3.  Find the official app in the search results, paying attention to the app icon and developer information.
4.  Tap the "Get" button. You may need to confirm the download via Face ID, Touch ID, or by entering your Apple ID password.

### Downloading on Windows/macOS Computers
For desktop operating systems, you typically need to download the installation package from the official website.
1.  Use a browser to visit the **official website** of Pegasus VPN.
2.  Look for sections like "Download" or "Client" on the website.
3.  Select the **Windows** or **macOS** version for download based on your computer system.
4.  After the download is complete, locate the installation file (usually a `.exe` file for Windows, `.dmg` for macOS), double-click it, and follow the on-screen prompts to complete the installation. Please read the options at each step carefully during installation.

## Initial Setup and Usage After Download

After successfully downloading and installing the app, you usually need to perform simple setup upon first opening:
1.  **Register/Log in to your account**: Open the Pegasus VPN app. You will need to log in using a registered email or phone number. If you don't have an account, please complete the registration process first.
2.  **Grant Permissions**: Grant the necessary network connection permissions as prompted by the app (e.g., creating a VPN configuration on Android, allowing VPN configuration addition on iOS).
3.  **Connect to a Server**: After logging in, you will typically see a "Connect" button or a server list. You can choose "Smart Connect" (automatically selects the optimal server) or manually select a specific country or region server from the list, then click connect.
4.  **Verify Connection**: After a successful connection, the app interface and notification bar will usually show a prompt. You can visit a website or service that is typically geo-restricted to test if the VPN is working properly.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What to do if prompted "Cannot Install" or "Installation Package Damaged" during download?
This is usually caused by an incomplete download of the installation file or device compatibility issues.
*   **Solution**: First, try clearing your browser's cache data, then download it again from the official source. For Android APK files, ensure the download is complete. If the problem persists, please visit the official website to check if your device model and system version are within the supported range.

### What if the antivirus software reports a virus for the installation package downloaded from the official website?
Some VPN clients might be falsely flagged by sensitive antivirus software due to their low-level network driver characteristics.
*   **Solution**: Please ensure your installation package is 100% sourced from the official website. If you confirm the source is reliable, you can add the installation package or its installation directory to the "Trusted" or "Exclusion" list in your antivirus software. Of course, always keep your antivirus software enabled to protect other parts of your system.

### Can't find the Pegasus VPN app in the App Store or Google Play?
This might be because the app is temporarily unavailable in your region's store, or the search keywords are inaccurate.
*   **Solution**: Try searching using the exact English name, or visit the official Pegasus VPN website directly. The official website usually provides direct links to the official app stores. If you still cannot find it, please contact Pegasus VPN's official customer service for the latest download instructions.

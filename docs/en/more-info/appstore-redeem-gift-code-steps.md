# Steps to Redeem Gift Codes on the App Store

## Complete Guide to Redeeming App Store Gift Codes

App Store gift codes are a popular way for Apple users to gift digital content to each other. Whether it's apps, in-app purchases, music, movies, or e-books from Apple Books, a single gift code can easily convey your thoughts. Mastering the correct redemption steps allows you or your friends to immediately enjoy the fun of digital gifts.

### Types of Gift Codes and How to Obtain Them

First, understanding the type of gift code you have is crucial. Apple primarily offers two types:
1.  **App Store & iTunes Gift Codes**: This is the most common type, usable for purchasing apps, games, subscription services (like Apple Music, Apple TV+), movies, music, and books. It usually has a fixed denomination (e.g., 50 CNY, 100 CNY).
2.  **Specific Content Gift Codes**: The giver can directly purchase a specific app, game, or album as a gift to send to you. You will receive an email containing a unique redemption link.

Gift codes can be purchased from the Apple website, authorized retailers (like supermarkets, convenience stores), or Apple retail stores.

### How to Redeem a Gift Code on iPhone or iPad

This is the most commonly used redemption method. Please follow these steps:

1.  **Open the App Store App**: Ensure you are logged into the correct Apple ID account.
2.  **Tap Your Profile Picture**: In the top right corner of the App Store homepage, tap your circular profile picture or initials.
3.  **Go to the Redemption Page**: On the account page that pops up, find and tap the "Redeem Gift Card or Code" option. You can also directly tap the "Today" tab at the bottom, then tap the profile picture at the top to find the same option.
4.  **Enter the Code**:
    *   **Physical Card**: If you have a physical card, you can use your device's camera to scan the coated code on the back of the card. The system will automatically recognize it.
    *   **Digital Code**: If you received a digital code (a string of alphanumeric characters), select "Enter Code Manually" and carefully type or paste the code. **Pay attention to case sensitivity and avoid entering spaces**.
5.  **Tap "Redeem"**: The system will verify the code. Upon success, the balance will be immediately added to your Apple ID account. Now, you can use this balance to make purchases in the App Store, iTunes Store, or any other Apple service.

### Redeeming Gift Codes on Mac or PC

If you prefer to operate on a computer, the process is equally simple:
*   **On Mac**: Open the **App Store** application. Find and click your Apple ID name at the bottom of the left sidebar, then select "View Information." In the account settings page, find the "Redeem Gift Card" option.
*   **On PC (Windows)**: Open the **iTunes** application (please note, Apple no longer updates iTunes for Windows, but older versions are still usable). After logging in, select "Account" > "Redeem" from the menu bar, then enter your gift code.

### Redemption Failed? Common Reasons and Solutions

If you encounter problems during the redemption process, please check the following points:
*   **Region Mismatch**: Gift codes have strict regional restrictions. For example, a gift code purchased in Mainland China can only be redeemed with an App Store account from Mainland China. **If your Apple ID is for another region (e.g., US, Japan), you will need a gift code for that corresponding region**. For a detailed guide on how to register and manage accounts for other regions, you can refer to this guide: [How to Register and Top Up Foreign Region App Store Accounts](/network/account-registration-guide/appstore-registration-and-top-up-methods).
*   **Code Already Used**: Each gift code can only be redeemed once.
*   **Input Error**: Carefully check the code. Common confusing characters include the number "0" and the letter "O", or the number "1" and the letter "I".
*   **Payment Method Issue**: Ensure your account information (such as billing address) is complete and valid. Sometimes the system requires verification of this information.
*   **Network Issue**: Try switching networks (e.g., from Wi-Fi to cellular data) and attempt again.

If you have ruled out the above issues and still cannot redeem, it is recommended to contact Apple's official customer support for assistance.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Does the balance expire after redeeming a gift code?
In most regions, including Mainland China, the account balance from redeeming an App Store gift code **does not have an expiration date** and can be kept permanently until used up. However, please note that laws in some countries or regions may stipulate that balances have an expiration date. Please refer to the local terms and conditions.

### Can I withdraw or transfer the balance from a gift code?
**No**. The balance from redeeming an App Store gift code can only be used for consumption within Apple's ecosystem, including purchasing apps, media content, subscription services, and paying for iCloud storage. The balance cannot be converted to cash or transferred to another Apple ID account.

### What should I do if I receive the message "This code is only valid for the China store"?
This message clearly indicates a regional restriction issue. It means the country/region of your currently logged-in Apple ID does not match the applicable region of the gift code. You need to log in to the device with a China region Apple ID to redeem it, or purchase a gift code for the corresponding region for your foreign region account.

# Steps to Redeem App Store Gift Codes

## How to Redeem an App Store Gift Code?

App Store gift codes (also known as gift cards or redemption codes) are a convenient way to obtain App Store and iTunes Store credit, subscribe to services, or access specific content (such as apps, in-app purchases, movies, music, books). Whether you receive a physical card or a digital code, the redemption process is simple and quick. Once successfully redeemed, the credit will be deposited into your Apple ID account and can be used for various purchases within the Apple ecosystem.

## Detailed Redemption Step-by-Step Guide

The redemption process is mainly divided into two scenarios: **Redeeming to Account Balance** and **Redeeming Specific Content**. Below are the detailed operational steps.

### Scenario One: Redeem to Account Balance (Most Common)

This is the most common practice, converting the gift code's face value into account balance for future flexible use.

1.  **Open the Redemption Entry**:
    *   On iPhone, iPad, or iPod touch: Open the **App Store** app. Scroll to the bottom, tap your Apple ID profile picture or name, then select "**Redeem Gift Card or Code**". You can also directly tap the "Today" tab at the bottom, then tap the profile picture in the top right corner to enter the account page and find the same option.
    *   On Mac: Open the **App Store** app. Find and click your name at the bottom of the sidebar, then select "**Redeem Gift Card**".
    *   On Windows PC via iTunes: Open iTunes, select "Account" from the top menu bar > "**Redeem**".

2.  **Enter the Redemption Code**:
    *   **Scan with Camera**: This is the most convenient method. Completely scratch off the silver coating on the back of the physical gift card to ensure the code is clear. Point your device's camera at the code, and the system will automatically recognize and fill it in. Ensure good lighting and clear focus.
    *   **Manual Entry**: If scanning is not possible, tap "You can also enter your code manually." Carefully enter the code (usually a 16-character combination of uppercase letters and numbers, without spaces). It's recommended to double-check to avoid failure due to confusing characters like "0" and "O", "1" and "I".

3.  **Confirm and Complete**:
    *   Tap the "Redeem" button. The system may prompt you to enter your Apple ID password for verification.
    *   Upon successful redemption, the screen will display a confirmation message, and your account balance will update immediately. You can view your current balance at the top of your account page in the App Store.

### Scenario Two: Redeem Specific Content or Subscriptions

Sometimes, codes are for redeeming specific apps, game bundles, or media content (like a movie).

1.  **Get the Dedicated Link**: This type of code usually comes with a specific redemption URL (e.g., `https://redeem.apple.com/redeem`).
2.  **Open the Link on Your Device**: Open the link in the Safari browser on your iPhone, iPad, or Mac.
3.  **Sign In and Redeem**: The system will guide you to sign in with your Apple ID and automatically add the specific content to your account library. If it's an app or in-app purchase, it might start downloading or take effect the next time you open the game.

## Important Tips and Notes

*   **Regional Restrictions**: Gift codes are **strictly bound** to the store region of your Apple ID. For example, a gift code for the China region can only be redeemed on a China-region Apple ID, and a US-region code can only be used for a US-region ID. If you have a foreign-region account and need to top up, you can refer to our detailed guide on [Foreign App Store Registration Methods and Top-up Methods](/network/account-registration-guide/appstore-registration-and-top-up-methods).
*   **Code Status**: Ensure the code has not been used before and is within its validity period (usually printed on the card).
*   **Account Security**: Always redeem through the official App Store or iTunes channels. Never enter your code on untrusted websites.
*   **Balance Usage**: Account balance can be used to purchase apps, in-app purchases, subscribe to services like Apple Music, iCloud+, Apple TV+, as well as buy movies, music, and books.
*   **Failed Redemption**: If you receive prompts like "This code is not valid" or "Cannot be used in this country or region," first confirm if the code matches your Apple ID region. If they match and it still fails, you can contact Apple Support for assistance.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Where can I view my balance after redeeming a gift code?
After successful redemption, open the App Store app, tap the "Today" tab at the bottom, then tap your profile picture in the top right corner to enter the account page. Your current balance will be prominently displayed at the top of the page, below your name.

### What should I do if I get a "Code is invalid" prompt during redemption?
Please troubleshoot following these steps:
1.  **Check Region**: This is the most common reason. Confirm that your Apple ID's store country/region exactly matches the gift code's issuing region.
2.  **Verify Input**: Carefully check the code, especially easily confused characters (like 0/O, 1/I), and ensure there are no extra spaces.
3.  **Confirm Status**: Confirm that the code is being used for the first time and has not expired.
4.  **Try Another Device**: Sometimes it might be a temporary network or cache issue. Try redeeming on another Apple device or computer.

### Can gift codes be used for Family Sharing?
**Yes, but with conditions**. Your App Store account balance **cannot** be directly shared with family members. However, if you use your account balance to purchase an app, music, or movie, and have "Purchase Sharing" enabled, then family members can download and use the content you've purchased. Additionally, as the family organizer, you can use your own balance to pay for subscription fees like iCloud+ for family members.

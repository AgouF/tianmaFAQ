# Steps to Redeem Gift Codes on the App Store

## Complete Guide to Redeeming App Store Gift Codes

App Store gift codes are an official Apple top-up method. Users can redeem codes to add balance to their Apple ID account for purchasing apps, in-app purchases, subscription services, or media content. Whether you receive a gift code from a friend or obtain one through a promotion, the redemption process is simple and quick. Below are detailed steps and important notes.

### How to Redeem an App Store Gift Code

You can complete the redemption on various devices such as iPhone, iPad, Mac, or iTunes on a PC. Here, we use the most common iPhone as an example:

1.  **Open the App Store App**: On your iOS device, find and tap the red "App Store" icon.
2.  **Go to Your Account Page**: Tap your profile picture or avatar icon in the top-right corner of the screen to enter your account page.
3.  **Find the Redemption Entry**: On the account page, find and tap the "Redeem Gift Card or Code" option. Sometimes this option may also appear as "Redeem Gift Card".
4.  **Enter the Redemption Code**:
    *   You can choose to use your device's camera to scan the scratched-off code on the back of the gift card.
    *   Alternatively, manually tap "Enter Code Manually" and carefully input the characters from the gift code. Please pay attention to case sensitivity and avoid confusing similar characters like the number "0" and the letter "O".
5.  **Complete the Redemption**: Tap the "Redeem" button in the top-right corner of the screen. The system will verify the code's validity. Upon success, the corresponding amount will be immediately added to your Apple ID balance. You can view the updated balance at the top of your account page.

**Redemption Methods on Other Devices**:
*   **On a Mac**: Open the App Store app, click your account name in the bottom-left corner, and select "Redeem Gift Card".
*   **On iTunes for PC**: Open iTunes, select "Account" > "Redeem" from the menu bar, then enter the code.

### Important Precautions Before Redeeming

To avoid redemption failure or issues, please be sure to note the following points:

*   **Check Code Validity**: Ensure the gift code is from Apple or an authorized channel and has not been used before. Each code can only be redeemed once.
*   **Verify Region Match**: **This is the most critical point.** The App Store gift code **must exactly match** the store region of your Apple ID. For example, a US region gift code can only be redeemed with a US region Apple ID account and cannot be used with a China region account. If you need to top up an account from a different region, you may need to first register an Apple ID for that region. For a detailed guide on how to register an account for a foreign region, you can refer to this guide: [How to Register and Top Up a Foreign Region App Store Account](/network/account-registration-guide/appstore-registration-and-top-up-methods).
*   **Check the Account Logged In on Your Device**: Before redeeming, confirm that the Apple ID currently logged into the App Store is the target account you wish to top up. The redemption operation is irreversible, and the funds will be directly added to the currently logged-in account.
*   **Network Connection**: Ensure your device is connected to a stable and reliable network to complete the verification process.

### Using and Managing Gift Code Balance

After successful redemption, the balance will be stored in your Apple ID. When you make any purchase (such as downloading a paid app, making an in-app purchase, subscribing to Apple Music or iCloud+), the system will first deduct the amount from your gift card balance. Any remaining balance due will be charged to your linked payment method.

You can check your remaining balance anytime in "Settings" > "[Your Name]" > "Media & Purchases" > "View Account" > "Account Balance". Gift card balances do not expire and can be used indefinitely.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What to do if prompted "Code Invalid" or "Unable to Redeem" during redemption?
First, carefully check if the entered characters are completely correct, including letter case. Secondly, this is the most common reason: **Please confirm whether your Apple ID country/region matches the gift code's issuing region.** For example, you cannot redeem a Japanese store gift code with a China region account. Finally, ensure the gift code itself is not expired and has not been used.

### Can a gift code be used for Family Sharing members?
After a gift code is redeemed into an Apple ID's balance, **it cannot be directly transferred or shared with other members in the Family Sharing group**. However, if you have "Purchase Sharing" enabled, as the family organizer, you can use your own balance to pay for purchases for family members. Family members cannot directly use the organizer's account balance for their own purchases.

### What to do if the balance doesn't appear immediately after redemption?
Typically, the balance updates immediately. If it doesn't show, try fully closing the App Store app (swipe it away from the app switcher), then reopen it and check your account page. You can also wait a few minutes and check again. If the balance hasn't arrived for an extended period, you can contact Apple Support and provide proof of redemption for inquiry.

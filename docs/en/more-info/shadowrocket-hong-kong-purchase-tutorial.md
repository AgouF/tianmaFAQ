# Shadowrocket Hong Kong Region Purchase Tutorial

### Preparation: Registering a Hong Kong Region Apple ID

To purchase apps from the Hong Kong region, you first need a Hong Kong region Apple ID. This is the crucial first step.

1.  **Sign Out of Current Apple ID**: In the "Settings" app on your iPhone or iPad, tap your Apple ID at the top, then select "Sign Out".
2.  **Create New Account**: Visit the official Apple account registration page, or directly try to download a free app in the App Store and select "Create New Apple ID" when prompted to sign in.
3.  **Select Region**: During registration, be sure to select "Hong Kong" for "Country or Region".
4.  **Fill in Information**:
    *   **Payment Method**: Select "None". This is key to successfully registering a Hong Kong region ID.
    *   **Address and Phone Number**: You need to provide a valid Hong Kong address and phone number. You can use publicly available information from Hong Kong hotels, business centers, or universities (for registration verification only, no actual mail will be sent). The phone number format is 8 digits.
5.  **Verify Email**: After completing the information, Apple will send a verification email to your registered email address. Click the link in the email to complete verification.

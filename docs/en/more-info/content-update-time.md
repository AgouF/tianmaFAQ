# Content Update Timeliness

## Content Update Frequency Explanation

We understand that providing the latest and most accurate information is crucial to our users. Our content updates follow a rigorous process designed to balance timeliness, depth, and accuracy. In general, our core knowledge base and guide-type content undergo regular maintenance and updates, while news, developments, and data-driven information are adjusted promptly or frequently based on changes.

### Update Cycles for Main Content Types

Different types of content have different update strategies and frequencies:

1.  **Core Knowledge & Tutorials**: This content (e.g., product usage guides, in-depth analysis articles, methodologies) prioritizes stability and depth. We typically conduct a systematic review **quarterly**. Based on technological developments, user feedback, and internal testing, we revise, supplement, or rewrite content to ensure its long-term validity.
2.  **News & Industry Developments**: For dynamic information like market trends, policy changes, and significant events, our goal is a **timely response**. We usually publish or update related content within 24-72 hours after relevant information is confirmed, striving to provide you with first-hand information.
3.  **Data & Statistical Reports**: All content containing specific data, charts, or citations will clearly indicate the data source and cutoff date. When new authoritative data is released, we evaluate and update relevant articles **within one month** to ensure data currency.
4.  **User Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)**: We continuously monitor user inquiries and feedback. The FAQ section is supplemented and optimized **monthly** based on newly emerging high-frequency questions to better align with users' actual needs.

### How to Access the Latest Content

To ensure you always access the most current information, we recommend the following methods:

*   **Check Page Indicators**: At the end or in the sidebar of most articles, you will find the "Last Updated" date. This is the most direct way to gauge the freshness of that content.
*   **Subscribe to Update Notifications**: If you have a sustained interest in specific areas (e.g., technical blog, industry reports), we recommend subscribing to our relevant notifications to receive alerts as soon as updates occur.
*   **Use Site Search**: When you search for a topic, our system prioritizes displaying the most recently published or updated relevant content.

We are committed to immediately labeling or removing any outdated information that could be misleading once identified. The continuous optimization of content is at the core of our work.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How can I provide feedback if I find outdated information?
We greatly appreciate your help! You can use the "Feedback" button at the bottom of each article or contact us directly to point out specific issues and the information you believe is correct. Our content team will verify and update the information promptly. Your feedback is a vital driver of our improvement. You are also welcome to learn more about our content creation philosophy via the [☀️About](/about) page.

### Through which channels are content updates announced?
For major content updates or new article series, we typically announce them via the "Latest News" section on the official website, official social media accounts, and emails to subscribed users. Routine minor corrections and optimizations are reflected directly on the pages with an updated "Last Modified" date and are not announced separately.

### Is there a complete changelog for all content?
Currently, we do not provide a site-wide, detailed changelog for every single article. However, each independent article carries its own version history (i.e., the last updated date). For important product documentation or tutorials, we list major update histories and key changes in the article introduction or a dedicated version section.

# MAC & iOS Download and Usage Guide

## The most recommended app for MAC & iOS is "Shadowrocket"

### Preparations

1. An AppStore account for a foreign region (register yourself or purchase on Xianyu). Registration tutorial: [Account Registration Guide for Various Platforms](/network/account-registration-guide/)
2. An iOS or Mac device

### Steps

Log in with your foreign region account. ⚠️ For Mac's AppStore, you need to log out of your current AppStore account in **System Settings > Apple Account > Media & Purchases**.

⚠️ **Do not log purchased accounts into iCloud.**

After logging in, open the AppStore and search for "小火箭" or "shadowrocket."

Open the webpage where you purchased the VPN, and you will find an import method similar to the image below:

![](/images/6085da6e51eb.webp)

Select any option, copy the link, then open Shadowrocket. It will automatically read the clipboard and import the configuration. If it does not recognize your clipboard, click the "+" in the upper right corner of Shadowrocket and paste the subscription link you copied.

![](/images/84ff2292913f.webp)

# MAC & IOS Download and Usage Guide

## The most recommended app for MAC & IOS is "Shadowrocket"

### Preparations

1. **AppStore Foreign Region Account** (Register yourself or purchase on Xianyu)  
   Registration Tutorial: [Account Registration Guide for Various Platforms](https://www.notion.so/320940f7aeb180dba4f0cbcf4a47746a)

2. **IOS or Mac Device**

### Steps

- Log in to your foreign region account.  
  ⚠️ For Mac's AppStore, you need to log out of your current AppStore account in **System Settings > Apple Account > Media & Purchases**.

- **Do not log purchased accounts into iCloud.**

- After logging in, open the AppStore and search for "小火箭" or "Shadowrocket."

- Open the webpage where you purchased the VPN. You will find an import method similar to the image below:

  ![image.png](/images/7b52dce38045.webp)

- Select any option, copy the link, then open Shadowrocket. It will automatically read the clipboard and import the configuration. If it doesn't recognize your clipboard, click the "+" in the top-right corner of Shadowrocket and paste the subscription link you copied.

  ![image.png](/images/2d5270015c4b.webp)

- **Connectivity Test**: Test the current node latency.

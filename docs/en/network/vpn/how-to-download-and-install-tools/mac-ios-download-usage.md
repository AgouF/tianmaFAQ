# MAC & iOS Download and Usage Guide

## The most recommended app for MAC & iOS is "Shadowrocket"

### Preparations

1. An AppStore account from a foreign region (register yourself or purchase from platforms like Xianyu). Registration tutorial: [Account Registration Guide for Various Platforms](/network/account-registration-guide/)
2. An iOS or Mac device

### Steps

1. Log in with your foreign region account. ⚠️ For Mac's AppStore, you need to log out of your current AppStore account in **System Settings > Apple Account > Media & Purchases**.
2. **Important**: Do **not** log the purchased account into iCloud.
3. After logging in, open the AppStore and search for "小火箭" or "Shadowrocket".
4. Open the webpage where you purchased the VPN service. You will find an import method similar to the image below:

![](/images/9d546483c1eb.webp)

5. Select any option, copy the link, then open Shadowrocket. It will automatically read the clipboard and import the configuration. If it doesn't recognize your clipboard, click the "+" in the top-right corner of Shadowrocket and paste your subscription link.

![](/images/5efb9d025b8b.webp)

6. Perform a connectivity test to check the latency of the current node.

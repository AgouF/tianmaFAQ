# How to Register and Top Up on Foreign App Stores

## I. Registering "Hong Kong" or "US" Apple IDs

1. Visit [https://www.icloud.com/](https://www.icloud.com/) - Step 1

![](/images/c7d0cd556044.webp)

Click "Create Apple ID" and fill in your information.

![](/images/fac0f81e3fa4.webp)

![](/images/f6af087b7a76.webp)

After successful registration 👇

![](/images/e9beee1ef3fe.webp)

Go directly to the App Store on your phone and log in with your new account. Note: You may need to log in twice for it to succeed in the App Store. (Downloading free apps does not require a linked payment method.)

Since Shadowrocket is a paid app and we haven't set up a payment method yet (which requires a Mastercard or Visa card), we will first purchase a Hong Kong App Store gift card from Taobao or Xianyu.

Shadowrocket costs 22 HKD, so we just need a gift card with a value of 22 HKD or more.

![](/images/baeeba457545.webp)

Purchasing a 30 HKD card is fine.

![](/images/9f6aa81071f0.webp)

After receiving the gift card code, go into the App Store and redeem it.

![](/images/cc5b89483355.webp)

Enter your gift card code.

![](/images/8692fbe7a258.webp)

Simply redeem it. If you see the screen below, the redemption was successful.

![](/images/b1e07b129778.webp)

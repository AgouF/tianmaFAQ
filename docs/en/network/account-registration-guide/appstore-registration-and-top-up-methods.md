# How to Register for a Foreign App Store Account and Top-Up Methods

## I. Registering for "Hong Kong" or "US" Apple ID

1. Go to [https://www.icloud.com/](https://www.icloud.com/) – Step 1  
   ![image.png](/images/73e22133028f.webp)  

   Click "Create Apple ID" and fill in your information.  
   ![image.png](/images/1977d2610461.webp)  
   ![image.png](/images/69d4bc0a52a2.webp)  

   After successful registration 👇  
   ![image.png](/images/b129052e22a3.webp)  

   Go directly to the App Store on your phone and log in with your new account.  
   **Note:** You may need to log in twice to successfully access the App Store. (Downloading free apps does not require binding a payment method.)

   Since Shadowrocket requires a purchase and we haven’t set up a payment method yet, you’ll need a Mastercard or Visa card. Alternatively, you can purchase a Hong Kong App Store gift card from Taobao or Xianyu.  

   Shadowrocket costs 22 HKD, so you only need a gift card with a value of 22 HKD or more.  
   ![d7ccc000b682078caf0a0fc8297f9c09.jpg](/images/664722f5a6b4.webp)  

   Purchasing a 30 HKD gift card is sufficient.  
   ![ea74119aa3306090c2ea857f81e10755.jpg](/images/909464f13633.webp)  

   After receiving the gift card code, go to the App Store and redeem it.  
   ![f92d7668430882e30671a72fd587b747.jpg](/images/9a8328e9b0a2.webp)  

   Enter your gift card code.  
   ![8515eb1be390ec09883d31d16b09c97a.jpg](/images/2eec2dbcbdae.webp)  

   Simply redeem it. The screen below indicates a successful redemption.  
   ![ce806f5be100374ca21373b3572dda13.jpg](/images/9d5d011009b6.webp)

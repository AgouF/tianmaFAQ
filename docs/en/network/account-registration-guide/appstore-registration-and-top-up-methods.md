# How to Register for an App Store Account in Other Regions and Top-Up Methods

## I. Registering "Hong Kong" or "US" Apple IDs

1. Visit [https://www.icloud.com/](https://www.icloud.com/) - Step 1

![image.png](/images/999f5938422f.webp)

Click "Create Apple ID" and fill in your information.

![image.png](/images/5ebe627fe7eb.webp)

![image.png](/images/2675861de1f3.webp)

After successful registration 👇

![image.png](/images/704f482afc7f.webp)

Go directly to the App Store on your phone and log in with your new account. Note: You may need to log in twice to successfully access the App Store. (Downloading free apps does not require binding a payment method.)

Since Shadowrocket requires a purchase and we haven't bound a payment method yet—which requires a Mastercard or Visa card—we will first purchase a Hong Kong App Store gift card from Taobao or Xianyu.

Shadowrocket costs 22 HKD, so we only need to buy a gift card with a value of 22 HKD or more.

![d7ccc000b682078caf0a0fc8297f9c09.jpg](/images/649f9f01d972.webp)

Purchasing a 30 HKD card is sufficient.

![ea74119aa3306090c2ea857f81e10755.jpg](/images/96cc957d7d4c.webp)

After receiving the gift card code, go to the App Store and redeem the gift code.

![f92d7668430882e30671a72fd587b747.jpg](/images/7e8369a32d6e.webp)

Enter your gift code.

![8515eb1be390ec09883d31d16b09c97a.jpg](/images/6a3c52667df9.webp)

Simply redeem it. If you see the following interface, the redemption was successful.

![ce806f5be100374ca21373b3572dda13.jpg](/images/f72b004f716a.webp)

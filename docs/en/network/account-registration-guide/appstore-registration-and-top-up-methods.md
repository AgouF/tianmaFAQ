# How to Register and Top Up on Foreign App Stores

## I. Registering "Hong Kong" or "US" Apple IDs

1. Visit [https://www.icloud.com/](https://www.icloud.com/) - Step 1

![](/images/1bec838ec493.webp)

Click "Create Apple Account" and fill in your information.

![](/images/f09dc4d3ee73.webp)

![](/images/2efa10012f1a.webp)

After successful registration 👇

![](/images/6c419c54ee9d.webp)

Go directly to the App Store on your phone and log in with your new account. Note: You may need to log in twice to successfully access the App Store. (Downloading free apps does not require a linked payment method.)

Since Shadowrocket requires a purchase and we haven't set up a payment method yet (which requires a Mastercard or Visa), we will first purchase a Hong Kong App Store gift card from Taobao or Xianyu.

The price of Shadowrocket is 22 HKD, so we only need to buy a gift card with a value of 22 HKD or higher.

![](/images/dc26017e94d2.webp)

Purchasing a 30 HKD card is sufficient.

![](/images/359a4a42e7fb.webp)

After receiving the gift card code, go to the App Store and redeem it.

![](/images/39e6a079314c.webp)

Enter your gift card code.

![](/images/8fe0a73b3b4e.webp)

Simply redeem it. The following screen indicates a successful redemption.

![](/images/6d88c322a5b3.webp)

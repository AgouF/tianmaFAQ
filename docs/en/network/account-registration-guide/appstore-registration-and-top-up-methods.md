# How to Register and Top Up on Foreign App Stores

## I. Registering "Hong Kong" or "US" Apple IDs

1. Go to [https://www.icloud.com/](https://www.icloud.com/) – Step 1

![](/images/2b827944cc52.webp)

Click "Create Apple ID" and fill in your information.

![](/images/c4ba34607da3.webp)

![](/images/b8fdda48ad74.webp)

After successful registration 👇

![](/images/b472b7b6809d.webp)

Go directly to the App Store on your phone and log in with your new account. Note: You may need to log in twice to successfully access the App Store. (Downloading free apps does not require a linked payment method.)

Since Shadowrocket is a paid app and we haven't set up a payment method yet—which requires a Mastercard or Visa—we'll first purchase a Hong Kong App Store gift card from Taobao or Xianyu.

Shadowrocket costs 22 HKD, so we just need a gift card with a value of 22 HKD or more.

![](/images/02e9f1cf5837.webp)

Purchasing a 30 HKD card is fine.

![](/images/0fb61ea1d01b.webp)

After receiving the gift card code, go to the App Store and redeem it.

![](/images/2cb3e04df417.webp)

Enter your gift card code.

![](/images/760d88c97abe.webp)

Simply redeem it. If you see the screen below, the redemption was successful.

![](/images/f5631134b8a1.webp)

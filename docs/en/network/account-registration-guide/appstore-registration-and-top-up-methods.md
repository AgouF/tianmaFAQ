# How to Register for an App Store Account in Other Regions and Top-Up Methods

I. Registering "Hong Kong" or "US" Region IDs

1. Go to [https://www.icloud.com/](https://www.icloud.com/) - Step 1

![](/images/425ba435b15c.webp)

Click "Create Apple Account" and fill in your information.

![](/images/36a93c174e33.webp)

![](/images/77531547f3b0.webp)

After successful registration 👇

![](/images/ab01114270bf.webp)

Go directly to the App Store on your phone and log in with your new account. Note: You may need to log in twice to successfully access the App Store. (Downloading free apps does not require binding a payment method.)

Since Shadowrocket requires a purchase and we haven't bound a payment method yet—which requires a Mastercard or Visa card—we will first go to Taobao/Xianyu to purchase a Hong Kong region App Store gift card.

The price of Shadowrocket is 22 HKD, so we only need to buy a gift card with a value of 22 HKD or more.

![](/images/6935d7008348.webp)

Purchasing a 30 HKD card is fine.

![](/images/c6f14a01ba23.webp)

After receiving the card code, go to the App Store and redeem the gift code.

![](/images/2d53d36136c0.webp)

Enter your gift code.

![](/images/0904ffb7b8a6.webp)

Simply redeem it. If you see the following interface, the redemption was successful.

![](/images/814ae548f583.webp)

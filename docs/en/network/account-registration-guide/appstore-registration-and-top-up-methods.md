# How to Register and Top Up on Foreign App Stores

## I. Registering "Hong Kong" or "US" Apple IDs

1. Go to [https://www.icloud.com/](https://www.icloud.com/) – Step 1

![](/images/27eb2f4f55fc.webp)

Click "Create Apple ID" and fill in your information.

![](/images/06f8d7991dea.webp)

![](/images/8695a5c24a1a.webp)

After successful registration 👇

![](/images/561f33146e42.webp)

Go directly to the App Store on your phone and log in with your new account. Note: You may need to log in twice to successfully access the App Store. (Downloading free apps does not require a linked payment method.)

Since Shadowrocket requires a purchase and we haven't linked a payment method yet (which requires a Mastercard or Visa card), we will first purchase a Hong Kong App Store gift card from Taobao or Xianyu.

Shadowrocket costs 22 HKD, so we just need to buy a gift card with a value of 22 HKD or higher.

![](/images/7066d3afa040.webp)

Purchasing a 30 HKD card is fine.

![](/images/79fe034625c2.webp)

After receiving the gift card code, go to the App Store and redeem it.

![](/images/63d451486d0d.webp)

Enter your gift card code.

![](/images/2fb5c2505e9a.webp)

Simply redeem it. The following screen indicates a successful redemption.

![](/images/cd8daebc5e39.webp)

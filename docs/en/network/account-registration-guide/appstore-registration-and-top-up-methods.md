# How to Register and Top Up on Foreign App Stores

## I. Registering "Hong Kong" or "US" Apple IDs

1. Go to [https://www.icloud.com/](https://www.icloud.com/) - Step 1

![](/images/8a441a2a6067.webp)

Click "Create Apple ID" and fill in your information.

![](/images/3f3c87895250.webp)

![](/images/5f18d8c30fca.webp)

After successful registration 👇

![](/images/b351de2e7838.webp)

Go directly to the App Store on your phone and log in with your new account. Note: You may need to log in twice to successfully access the App Store. (Downloading free apps does not require binding a payment method.)

Since Shadowrocket requires a purchase and we haven't bound a payment method yet—which requires a Mastercard or Visa card—we will first go to Taobao or Xianyu to buy a Hong Kong App Store gift card.

Shadowrocket costs 22 HKD, so we just need to buy a gift card with a value of 22 HKD or more.

![](/images/6dff5c89c6f3.webp)

Buying a 30 HKD card is fine.

![](/images/4d0d41c3699c.webp)

After receiving the gift card code, go to the App Store and redeem it.

![](/images/b235ff3802cc.webp)

Enter your gift card code.

![](/images/aa1cf506fd89.webp)

Simply redeem it. If you see the screen below, the redemption was successful.

![](/images/2472f4657e82.webp)
